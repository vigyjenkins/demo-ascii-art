Yugi Oh

Yu-Gi-Oh! Trading Card Game

Yu-Gi-Oh! is a trading card game created by manga artist Kazuki Takahashi. The game is based on the fictional game of Duel Monsters, which was created by Takahashi in his manga series Yu-Gi-Oh!. The game has become one of the most popular trading card games in the world, with millions of players and collectors.

The game is played between two players, each using a deck of cards that represent monsters, spells, and traps. Players take turns drawing cards and playing them to summon monsters, cast spells, and activate traps in an effort to reduce their opponent's life points to zero.
